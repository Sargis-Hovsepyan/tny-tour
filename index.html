Good to know

Questions parents ask us

Still unsure about something? Message us — we answer every question personally.

How closely are the students supervised?

A 1:8 adult-to-student ratio at all times, with evening headcounts, room curfews and a coordinator on call 24/7. Students are never left to roam unsupervised.

How will I hear from my child during the trip?

We run a private parents' WhatsApp group with a daily photo update and check-in, and provide a dedicated emergency number staffed for the duration of the tour. Hotels have Wi-Fi.

Are flights included?

This is a land-only package, so international flights are arranged separately — we can advise on a recommended group flight and meeting point.

What should my child pack?

Comfortable walking shoes, layers (the mountains are cool even in spring), a light rain jacket, a refillable water bottle, and a Type C/F 230V adapter. A full packing list is sent on booking.

What about dietary needs and allergies?

Vegetarian, halal and allergy requirements are accommodated — just tell us on the medical & consent form collected before departure.

Do you need passports or visas?

Yes, a passport valid for at least 6 months. Visa requirements depend on nationality; we advise, but families are responsible for securing any visa.
