Why this tour

Everything a parent wants to hear

We designed this trip around the two things that matter most: your child's safety, and a week of real learning they'll never forget. TNY Educational Center has organized student programs since 2014 — this tour is our most ambitious yet.

From the Dracula legend at Bran Castle to the underground world of Slanic Salt Mine, from the fairy-tale Peleș Castle to the colossal Parliament in Bucharest — every day is structured, supervised, and designed to inspire.

Safe & supervised

1:8 chaperone ratio, 24/7 on-call coordinator, nightly headcounts. Daily photo updates to a private parents' group.

Real learning

Guided history at Bran Castle, Peleș Castle and Parliament. Underground science at the salt mine. Culture at Europe's most beautiful bookstore.

Everything handled

Flights, hotels, transport, meals, entrance fees and insurance in one clear price. No surprises on the road.

You stay in the loop

Private WhatsApp group with daily photos and check-ins, plus a dedicated 24/7 emergency line for the entire trip.

Good to know

Questions parents ask us

How closely are the students supervised?

A 1:8 adult-to-student ratio at all times, with evening headcounts, room curfews and a coordinator on call 24/7.

How will I hear from my child during the trip?

We run a private parents' WhatsApp group with a daily photo update, plus a dedicated emergency number. Hotels have Wi-Fi.

Are flights included?

Yes, return flights from Armenia to Bucharest are included. The group flies together with chaperones on both legs.

What should my child pack?

Comfortable walking shoes, warm layers (late October in Romania can be 5-15 C), a rain jacket, and a Type C/F 230V adapter. A full packing list is sent on booking.

What about dietary needs and allergies?

Vegetarian, halal and allergy requirements are accommodated — just tell us on the consent form before departure.

Do students need a passport or visa?

A passport valid for at least 6 months is required. Visa requirements depend on nationality — we advise on this.

What's the refund policy?

Contact us for full cancellation terms. We aim to be fair — exact deadlines and percentages are shared when you reserve.
