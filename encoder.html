Photo Encoder

A small offline helper to put your own photos into the brochure. It shrinks and compresses the photo, then gives you a block of text to paste into index.html. Nothing is uploaded anywhere — this runs entirely in your browser.

  1. Open index.html in a plain text editor (Notepad, TextEdit in plain-text mode, or VS Code).
  2. Press Ctrl/Cmd+F and search for the slot name in quotes, e.g. "bran": — it's in the IMAGES block near the bottom.
  3. Select everything from "bran": up to (but not including) the next comma, and replace it with the copied text.
  4. Save the file and re-open it in your browser. Done.

Tip: the copied text already includes the slot name and a trailing comma, so you can also just paste it on a new line inside the { } of the IMAGES block, replacing the old line for that slot.
